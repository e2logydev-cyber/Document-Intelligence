Phase 2 Outcome
Azure Development

Why AZ-900 matters for this POC

Before wiring Azure Blob Storage, Document Intelligence, AI Search, and OpenAI into the platform, every developer needs to understand how Azure resources are provisioned, billed, and governed. AZ-900 gives the team a shared language and prevents costly misconfiguration.

› Understand Azure resource groups and subscriptions used in Week 1 infra setup
› Azure Key Vault — storing OpenAI keys, connection strings, secrets
› Azure AD & identity — basis for Blazor Server AAD auth (Week 1)
› Azure pricing & FinOps — estimating monthly POC costs accurately
